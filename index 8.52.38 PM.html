    Procedure:

    1. Start by sanding.

    Be sure to start any painting project by sanding your surfaces with sandpaper. Be careful not to gouge the surface, you are just trying to rough up the surface so the primer has something to adhere to.

    2. Remove residue.

    After sanding, wipe down the surface with a tack cloth to remove any residue.

    3. Prime it.

    The next step is to prime using a mini foam roller or a foam brush to get into hard to reach areas. Let the primer dry according to the instructions on the can of primer. Once it's fully dry, sand away any drips with a sanding block, and wipe your furniture down with a tack cloth.

    4. Paint it!

    Use a mini foam roller to apply three thin coats of the paint of your choice. Let the paint dry for six to eight hours in between coats. It's crucial that you sand in between coats if you have any drips or residue on the piece to make sure your final product is perfect. Use the sanding block and a new tack cloth.

    5. Protect it.

    Use a new mini foam roller to apply a thin coat of water-based protectant gloss. Make sure you go over this coat very lightly with the foam roller to smooth any bubbles that occured. Allow the furniture 72 hours for it to dry before using.

    Warnings:

  • Do not use a paper towel, and don't speculate on whether it might be better to use a lint-free cloth.

  • Wait at least 72 hours for it to dry before using; otherwise, it can get sticky.

  • Be careful using the orbit sander, avoid placing your hands near the sanding head.

  • Paint in a well ventilated room or outside to avoid ingesting paint fumes.p>

    Our Commmitment to Inclusivity

    On Earth, each of us contributes a carbon footprint, and we all have a role to play. The state of the environment is the result of our behaviors, our neglect, our material items, how we dispose of these items, and the ways that we courageously share new ways to actively work towards the conservation of the Earth and encourage others to do the same. In turn, our furniture refurbishing manual inspires us to try new things, renovate our run down items into something worth keeping, and be environmentally conscious in a way that is fun and creative. Working to be more environmentally conscious brings us together in ways that help us stand out. Our furniture refurbishing manual empowers all of us to connect, work together, and change the world.
